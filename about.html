About Iloilo

Welcome to Discover Iloilo

Discover Iloilo is a simple yet meaningful project that aims to showcase the beauty, culture, and rich heritage of the Province of Iloilo. Known as the "Heart of the Philippines," Iloilo offers a perfect blend of historical landmarks, natural attractions, and warm hospitality.

This website highlights some of the most remarkable towns in Iloilo, including Miagao, Guimbal, Oton, San Joaquin, Tubungan, Tigbauan, Iloilo City, and Molo Church. Each place has its own unique story, from centuries-old churches and historical sites to scenic beaches and breathtaking landscapes.

Our Purpose

The goal of this website is to promote local tourism and increase awareness of Iloilo’s hidden gems. By presenting these destinations in a simple and visually appealing way, we hope to inspire visitors, students, and travelers to explore and appreciate the province more deeply.

Why Iloilo?

Iloilo stands out because of its rich cultural heritage, evident in its Spanish-era churches, festivals, and traditions. Beyond history, it is also home to natural wonders such as waterfalls, coastlines, and rolling landscapes that provide a peaceful escape from city life.

Whether you are looking for adventure, relaxation, or a deeper connection with Filipino culture, Iloilo has something to offer for everyone.
